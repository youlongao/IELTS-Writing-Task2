# Crime - IELTS Writing Task 2
# 犯罪

## 常见题型 (Common Question Types)

1. **Prison is the best way to punish criminals. To what extent do you agree or disagree?**
   监禁是惩罚罪犯的最佳方式。你在多大程度上同意或不同意？

2. **Some people believe that the death penalty is an effective deterrent to serious crime. Others argue it is inhumane and should be abolished. Discuss both views.**
   一些人认为死刑是对严重犯罪的有效威慑。另一些人则认为它不人道，应该废除。讨论双方观点。

3. **Young people who commit serious crimes should be punished in the same way as adults. Do you agree or disagree?**
   犯下严重罪行的年轻人应该与成年人一样受罚。你同意还是不同意？

4. **The best way to reduce crime is to install more surveillance cameras in public places. Do you agree?**
   减少犯罪的最佳方法是在公共场所安装更多监控摄像头。你同意吗？

5. **What are the causes of juvenile delinquency, and what measures can be taken to address this issue?**
   青少年犯罪的成因是什么，可以采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

6. **Rehabilitation should be the main purpose of the criminal justice system, not punishment. Discuss.**
   改造应是刑事司法系统的主要目的，而非惩罚。请讨论。

## 核心论点 (Core Arguments)

### 支持方论点 (Arguments For)

- **Argument: Harsh punishments serve as effective deterrents.**
  - Logic: Potential criminals weigh costs and benefits → Severe consequences tip the balance → Crime rates decline → Society enjoys greater safety → Fewer victims overall
  - 中文：潜在罪犯权衡成本和收益 → 严厉后果打破平衡 → 犯罪率下降 → 社会享有更大的安全 → 总体上受害者减少

- **Argument: Prison protects society by incapacitating dangerous individuals.**
  - Logic: Violent offenders removed from communities → Physical barrier prevents re-offending during sentence → Law-abiding citizens can live without fear → Prison serves a crucial containment function
  - 中文：将暴力罪犯从社区中移除 → 物理隔离防止在服刑期间再次犯罪 → 守法公民可以无恐惧地生活 → 监狱发挥着关键的遏制功能

- **Argument: Surveillance technology prevents crime proactively.**
  - Logic: Cameras create perception of being watched → Would-be criminals deterred by risk of identification → Police solve crimes faster with footage → Public spaces become safer for all
  - 中文：摄像头制造被注视的感知 → 潜在罪犯因被识别的风险而被震慑 → 警方借助录像更快破案 → 公共空间对所有人都更安全

- **Argument: Rehabilitation reduces recidivism more than punishment alone.**
  - Logic: Education and therapy address root causes → Offenders gain employable skills → Released with legitimate income path → Lower re-offending rates → Long-term cost savings for society
  - 中文：教育和治疗解决根本原因 → 罪犯获得就业技能 → 获释后有合法收入途径 → 再犯率降低 → 社会长期成本节约

### 反对方论点 (Arguments Against)

- **Argument: The death penalty risks executing innocent people.**
  - Logic: Judicial systems are fallible → Wrongful convictions proven by DNA evidence → Execution is irreversible → Moral cost of state-sanctioned killing of innocents is indefensible → Life imprisonment achieves same incapacitation without finality
  - 中文：司法系统会犯错 → DNA证据证明的错误定罪 → 死刑不可逆 → 国家许可杀害无辜者的道德代价是无可辩驳的 → 终身监禁达到同样的隔离效果且不可逆转

- **Argument: Treating juveniles as adults ignores developmental science.**
  - Logic: Adolescent brains are not fully developed → Impulse control and long-term thinking are impaired → Young offenders have greater capacity for change → Adult sentencing permanently derails lives → Society loses their potential contributions
  - 中文：青少年大脑未完全发育 → 冲动控制和长远思维受损 → 年轻罪犯有更大的改变潜力 → 成人刑罚永久性地阻断了他们的生活 → 社会失去了他们潜在的贡献

- **Argument: Mass surveillance undermines civil liberties.**
  - Logic: Pervasive monitoring creates a chilling effect on free expression → Data can be misused by authorities → Law-abiding citizens disproportionately affected → Democratic freedoms eroded in the name of security
  - 中文：无处不在的监控对自由表达产生寒蝉效应 → 数据可能被当局滥用 → 守法公民受到不成比例的影响 → 民主自由以安全之名被侵蚀

- **Argument: Punitive systems ignore root causes of crime.**
  - Logic: Poverty, inequality, and lack of opportunity drive crime → Punishment alone treats symptoms → Without addressing structural factors, new offenders continuously emerge → Prevention through social investment is more effective and cheaper
  - 中文：贫困、不平等和缺乏机会驱动犯罪 → 仅惩罚只是治标 → 不解决结构性因素，新罪犯会不断出现 → 通过社会投资进行预防更有效更便宜

## 典型例子 (Typical Examples)

1. **Norway's rehabilitation-focused prison system** — Bastoy Prison allows inmates to live in a village-like setting with jobs, education, and therapy; Norway has one of the lowest recidivism rates in the world at around 20%, compared to over 60% in the US. Demonstrates that humane treatment yields better outcomes.
   挪威以改造为重点的监狱系统——巴斯托伊监狱允许囚犯住在类似村庄的环境，有工作、教育和心理治疗；挪威的再犯率约为20%，是世界上最低之一，而美国超过60%。证明人道待遇产生更好结果。

2. **The exoneration of death row inmates** — In the US, over 190 people sentenced to death have been exonerated since 1973 through DNA evidence and other means. Each case represents someone the state would have wrongly executed. Powerful evidence against the death penalty.
   死刑犯的平反——自1973年以来，美国已有超过190名被判死刑的人通过DNA证据和其他方式被证明无罪。每个案例都代表了国家本会错误处决的人。反对死刑的有力证据。

3. **Singapore's strict laws and low crime rate** — Singapore employs harsh punishments including mandatory death penalty for drug trafficking and caning for vandalism; consistently ranks among the world's safest cities. Often cited by proponents of deterrence-based justice.
   新加坡的严格法律和低犯罪率——新加坡采用严厉惩罚，包括对贩毒的强制死刑和对破坏公物的鞭刑；始终名列全球最安全城市。常被威慑型司法支持者引用。

4. **Brazil's favela youth programs** — Community-based programs in Rio's slums provide music, sports, and vocational training to at-risk youth; significantly reduced gang recruitment and juvenile crime. Shows how prevention and opportunity can break cycles of crime.
   巴西贫民窟青年项目——里约贫民窟的社区项目为高危青少年提供音乐、体育和职业培训；显著减少了帮派招募和青少年犯罪。展示预防和机会如何打破犯罪循环。
